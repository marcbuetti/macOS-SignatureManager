Privacy Policy
Signature Manager for macOS
Last updated: January 2026


This application respects your privacy.
All data is processed locally on your Mac.
No personal information is collected, stored, or transmitted to any external servers or third parties without your organization’s explicit configuration.

Data Handling
The app processes and stores the following information locally:
- Signature and configuration settings
- Email accounts and related signatures within the macOS Mail app
- Device and system information required for signature management

By default, all data remains securely on your Mac.
If your organization provides a configuration profile, the following exceptions may apply:
- Connection to your organization’s Signature Cloud
- Synchronization of signatures with Microsoft 365 (if enabled by your organization)

In these cases, communication occurs only between your device and the services explicitly authorized by your organization.

By default, the app does not connect to any external servers or cloud services.
All signatures and configuration data remain on-device only.

Security
- Local data is stored securely in the macOS Keychain.
- Communication with the Signature Cloud or Microsoft 365 (if activated) is performed exclusively over HTTPS and uses secure authentication mechanisms defined by your organization.

Third-Party Services
Depending on your organization’s configuration, the application may use the following external services:
- Signature Cloud (only if enabled through a configuration profile)
- Microsoft 365 (only if authorized by your organization)

The use of these services is subject to the respective providers’ privacy policies.
